🌌 Explore Space

Our Solar System

The Solar System is made up of the Sun and everything that orbits around it – including planets, moons, asteroids, and comets. Let’s explore some highlights!

🌞 Sun

A massive ball of gas that gives us light and heat.

🌍 Earth

The only known planet to support life.

🌕 Moon

Earth’s natural satellite that controls tides.

🔴 Mars

The Red Planet with the tallest volcano in the solar system.

🪐 Saturn

Famous for its beautiful rings of ice and rock.

🔵 Neptune

The windiest planet with deep blue color.

💠 Uranus

Rotates on its side and has faint rings.

🟠 Jupiter

The biggest planet, home to the Great Red Spot storm.

🧊 Pluto

A dwarf planet in the Kuiper Belt – small, cold, and mysterious.

Why Explore Space?

Space exploration helps us understand our place in the universe, pushes technology forward, and inspires the next generation of scientists and dreamers.
